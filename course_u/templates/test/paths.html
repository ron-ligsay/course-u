Pre Assessment

Before entering their first year of college, users undergo an initial assessment to evaluate their knowledge and skills.

1st Year Assessment

Following the pre assessment, users input grades for 1st-year majors before proceeding to the next assessment.

2nd Year Assessment

Following the 1st year assessment, users input grades for 2nd-year majors before proceeding to the next assessment.

3rd Year Assessment

After the 2nd year assessment, users input grades for 3rd-year majors before taking another assessment.

4th Year Assessment

After 3rd year assessment, users input their grades for 4th-year majors and will recommend career based on the results.
