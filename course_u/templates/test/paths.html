A Guide to Course University

Introduction

Course University: A career recommendation system caters to a diverse range of users, offering personalized guidance and recommendations to support PUP CCIS students in making well-informed decisions about their educational and professional journeys.

Course U Features

  • Assessments

    > Users will input their information such as their grades, course, and year.

    > Users will take the assessment to evaluate their skills.

    > Users will continue the assessment process until they graduate.

  • Generate Results

    > The system will generate the result based on the assessment.

    > The system will provide transparency and records of the result.

  • Generate Recommendations

    > The system will generate the Top 3 field recommendations and provide transparency of the results.

    > The system will also provide a roadmap, jobs, and specializations after the recommendation.

  • Provide Learning Materials

    > Learning materials, resources, and links are provided after the recommendation.

    > Tracking of previous activity and jobs are also available in the system.

  • Recommend Jobs

    > The system will generate job recommendations based on the result of the assessment.

    > Users will be redirected to LinkedIn once they click apply.


STUDENT GUIDELINES

Pre Assessment

Before entering their first year of college, freshmen users will undergo an initial assessment to evaluate their knowledge and skills that will serve as their guide.

1st Year Assessment

Following the pre assessment, users will input their grades for 1st-year majors before proceeding to the next assessment.

2nd Year Assessment

Following the 1st year assessment, users input grades for 2nd-year majors before proceeding to the next assessment.

3rd Year Assessment

After the 2nd year assessment, users will input grades for their 3rd-year majors before taking their last assessment.

4th Year Assessment

After 3rd year assessment, users input their grades for 4th-year majors and will recommend career based on the results.
